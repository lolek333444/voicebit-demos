Frequently asked questions

How quickly can VoiceBit be set up for my restaurant?
Phone AI: as little as 3 hours. Web ordering: 1–5 days. We configure your menu, train the AI on your offerings, and ensure everything works before going live — with zero disruption to your operations.
What happens if VoiceBit can't handle a customer's request?
VoiceBit seamlessly transfers calls to your staff when needed — with full context so the customer never has to repeat themselves. You stay in complete control.
Can VoiceBit integrate with my existing POS system?
Yes. VoiceBit integrates directly with Toast, Square, Clover, and many other POS systems. We handle the entire setup so orders flow directly into your existing workflow from day one.
What languages does the AI support?
English and Spanish today, with more languages on the roadmap. The AI switches naturally mid-conversation based on how the customer speaks.
Can I offer delivery without using DoorDash or Uber Eats?
Yes. VoiceBit enables direct delivery ordering through both phone and web — you keep 100% of food revenue. The system handles orders, payments, and routing automatically.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — month-to-month, no long-term contracts, no cancellation penalties. We're confident you'll love the results, and we want you to stay because VoiceBit works.
Can I offer delivery to my customers?
Yes, VoiceBit provides you with the ability to offer direct delivery across phone and web to your customers without shedding any margin and retaining 100% of the food revenue. The system captures orders, collects payments and routes delivery without you having to lift a finger.
